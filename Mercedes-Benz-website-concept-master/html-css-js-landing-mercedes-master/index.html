The VISION AVTR

Inspired by the future

concept

Electromobility in harmony with man and nature. Eco-friendly organic battery technology and thoughtful operational strategy of the VISION AVTR concept car.

premier

At CES 2020, a new concept Mercedes-Benz was presented - VISION AVTR. Futuristic concept The presented electric vehicle unites man, nature and the car in a unique way.

Ola Källenius, Chairman of the Board of Management of Daimler AG and Mercedes-Benz AG, presented the concept car in his speech together with Academy Award winner and Avatar creator James Cameron.

It was the philosophy created by the cult director in the science fiction film that became the basis for the creation of VISION AVTR.

“Mercedes-Benz has always been one of the most technologically advanced premium brands. Now it's time to combine luxury and environmental friendliness. After all, this is the only way we can meet the standards of the future.”

INTERIOR & EXTERIOR

Дизайн VISION AVTR – воплощение экологичности

The innovative concept of VISION AVTR combines an eco-friendly interaction between interior, exterior and UX design. All the design process was focused on a specific result - a unique experience of interaction and perception concept car driver and passengers. It was about creating a unique space in which passengers have biometric connection with each other, with the vehicle and the outside world.

The side outer hole passes through the inner part and creates an endless loop, the prototype of which was the sacred connection between the Na'vi people in the film Avatar and their natural habitat.

Also, thanks to the elongated "One Bow" design and organic design philosophy, the VISION AVTR demonstrates a radical a new stage in the automotive industry. Thus, having gone from the concept of awareness to the unique fusion of man and car, Mercedes-Benz engineers managed to create the VISION AVTR concept electric car.

A laconic continuation of the external minimalist design are the front seats, made in a very organic shape that resembles leafy hammocks from the planet Pandora. The center console symbolizes the Tree of Souls, the most... Na'vi sacred place. The control unit is intuitive and incredibly functional.

In addition, the control center is located in such a way that it is easily accessible to both the driver and the driver. passenger, as well as for rear passengers.

In this way, VISION AVTR establishes a close biometric connection with the driver and helps to increase awareness surrounding situation.

The back of the seats and the interior of the roof are made of a special fabric that changes color. Depending on the lighting textiles can be dark blue or delicate light blue.

But besides this, the environmental friendliness of the electric vehicle interior is enhanced with the help of DINAMICA® vegetarian leather - the first and the only microfiber that guarantees environmental performance throughout the entire production cycle.

DINAMICA® is recycled material from old clothes, fabrics and PET plastic bottles.
